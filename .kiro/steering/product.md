# Product Overview

**Healing Specialist** is a healthcare platform service focused on medical specialist management and patient empowerment.

## Core Purpose
- Manages medical specialist profiles and credentials
- Enables specialists to share consultation reports directly with patients
- Provides patient discovery of healthcare providers through searchable profiles
- Validates professional licenses through external integrations

## Key Domain Concepts
- **Specialist**: Licensed healthcare professionals with verified credentials
- **Patient Data Ownership**: Specialists agree to share medical records directly with patients
- **Professional Verification**: External license validation for credibility
- **Discoverability**: Keyword-based specialist search and matching

## Business Rules
- All specialists must have valid professional licenses (e.g., CRM in Brazil)
- Specialists must explicitly agree to share medical reports with patients
- Each specialist maintains independent availability and pricing (handled by separate services)
- Focus on immutable identity and credentials, not dynamic scheduling data
